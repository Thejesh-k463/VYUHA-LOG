The difference

Most journals are a spreadsheet with charts.

Vyuha is a local-first trading back-office. Six things nothing else on the Indian market does properly.

🇮🇳

Charges to the rupee

STT, exchange txn, SEBI, stamp, IPFT, GST, DP, pledge — computed per broker × segment × exchange from an editable rate table and reconciled against real broker files. Money is stored as integer paise, so there is no float drift, ever.

💸

MTF done right

Interest accrues on the broker-funded portion only, with the correct T+1 day count. Tools that assume the full position value quietly overstate your cost. See real ROI on your own capital — and a warning when interest has eaten your entire paper gain.

🪜

Positions built in tranches

Scale in with a stop on each entry, scale out in parts, see per-leg R. Every other journal models a trade as one entry and one exit — which is not how anyone actually trades.

📋

Knows today's SEBI regime

A compliance radar reads your open book against the post-2024 F&O rules — the +2% expiry-day ELM on short options, the loss of calendar-spread benefit on expiry, which indices still have weeklies, and how close you sit to the ₹1,500 cr index limit now snapshotted intraday.

🔍

Analytics that don't flatter you

Expectancy cards warn you when the sample is too small to trust. The stop-tuning report says "descriptive, not prescriptive." Mistake reports show the expectancy gap — never a fabricated counterfactual P&L designed to make you feel better.

🔒

Nothing leaves your machine

No login. No cloud. No telemetry. No analytics SDKs. Everything lives in one SQLite file on your disk — copy it and you have backed up your entire trading life.

Questions

Straight answers.

Does my trade data ever leave my computer?

No. There is no account, no server and no telemetry. Everything lives in a single SQLite file on your disk. The only network activity is optional and explicit: checking for an update, and the opt-in end-of-day bhavcopy download for mark-to-market prices. Both can be left off.

What is free, and what am I paying for?

The core journal is free forever — recording trades, importing broker files, the dashboard, playbooks and backups. Your own record of your trading is never held hostage. Payment unlocks the analytics layer: the portfolio risk cockpit, the tax suite, the ITR pack and broker cost comparison.

Which brokers can I import from?

Zerodha (tradebook and Console P&L), Dhan, Groww, Angel One and Upstox — tradebook or aggregated P&L exports — plus PDF statements. Files are auto-detected and de-duplicated, and charges are recomputed from your own rate card so you can see exactly where your broker's number differs.

Can I move it to a new computer?

Yes. Copy your database file across and re-paste your key. If your key was locked to a specific machine at your request, message us with the new Machine ID shown in Settings → License and we will re-issue it. Windows desktop only for now.

Do the indicators tell me what to buy?

No — and be wary of anything that claims to. They are analytical and educational tools that visualise market structure. Vyuha does not place, close or modify any order, and makes no prediction about future prices. Please read the disclaimer below.

Is there a refund policy?

You get 14 days of the complete Pro feature set before paying anything, which is longer and more honest than most refund windows. Message us on WhatsApp if something is not working — we would much rather fix it than take money for a tool you cannot use.
