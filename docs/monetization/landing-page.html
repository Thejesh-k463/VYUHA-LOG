Questions, answered

Is my data really private?

Yes. Vyuha runs entirely on your PC with a local database. There's no account, no login, and nothing is uploaded — it works with your internet off.

Which brokers can I import?

Zerodha (tradebook / Console P&L), Dhan (CSV), Groww (XLSX) and broker PDF statements today — plus manual entry for anything else.

How do I get the indicators after paying?

At checkout you'll enter your TradingView username. We grant invite-only access to both indicators, usually within a few hours, and email you confirmation.

Is this a one-time payment?

Yes — pay once, own it, with lifetime updates. No recurring charges.

Does it work on Mac?

Currently Windows 10/11 (64-bit). Mac is on the roadmap.

Is this investment advice?

No. Vyuha is a record-keeping and analytics tool and the indicators are educational tools. Nothing here is a buy/sell recommendation. See the disclaimer below.
